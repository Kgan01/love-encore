QR Code Best Practices

  • Size: Minimum 1" x 1" for reliable scanning
  • Contrast: Dark blue (#0e4fc4) on white background
  • Quiet Zone: Leave white space around the QR code
  • Testing: Always test before mass production
  • Call to Action: Include "Scan to Schedule" or similar text
  • Backup: Include short URL when possible
